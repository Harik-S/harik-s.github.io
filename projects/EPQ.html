This project was about sustainable aircraft design and was in 2 parts. The first involved researching various things, with full analysis and evaluation of sources. The second part was a design project, where I modified wing design and tried to optimise for sustainability. I used SolidWorks CFD. However, in the conclusions, I discussed improvements including Bayesian Optimisation and Ansys + SolidWorks API. The method used was grid search and this resulted in very slow runs, because varying 5 or so different parameters across 20 different values gives a very large number of combinations. This could be used alongside other tools to optimise even better. The full report can be found here.
